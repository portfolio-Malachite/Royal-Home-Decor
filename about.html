About Us

Crafting elegant homes with warmth, detail, and a refined eye.

Royal Home Decor is a luxury interior design studio shaping timeless homes through tailored concepts, premium materials, and beautifully resolved spaces.

“Our goal is to make every room feel balanced, personal, and beautifully complete.”

Aarav Sharma Founder & Creative Director
Our Story

From first sketch to final styling, we design with precision and feeling.

Founded over a decade ago, Royal Home Decor began with a simple belief: luxury should never feel cold. We create homes that are sophisticated, inviting, and deeply aligned to the people who live in them.

Our process combines spatial planning, custom detailing, and a layered styling approach so each project feels polished yet natural. We treat every home as a complete experience, not just a collection of rooms.
